With over ten million subscribers, The New York Times is one of the most successful digital newspapers in the world. The approximately one thousand seven hundred journalists write over forty thousand articles a year on the topics that move us.

In order to gain a deeper understanding of the topics currently occupying the world, we used the Times Wire API to create a news live ticker and various data visualizations.

With the help of an evaluation of the most popular keywords and published articles per category, we tried to identify the current trends. In addition, another data visualization shows which days were particularly eventful based on the number of articles published.
